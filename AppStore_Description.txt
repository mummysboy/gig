Gig is the smart phonebook for the gig economy.

Whether you're looking to hire or get hired, Gig is the fastest, simplest way to connect. Use your face to log in securely, tell the app what you need in a quick chat, and get instant access to verified, local service providers.

Key Features:
- Face ID–only login (no passwords!)
- Smart, chat-based service search
- In-app VoIP calling between clients and providers
- Public provider profiles with voice notes, photos, and availability
- Location-based matching for local gigs
- Feedback collection after each call

Perfect for handymen, tutors, cleaners, creatives, coaches, and anyone offering services.
